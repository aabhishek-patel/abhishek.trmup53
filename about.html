About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I am an enthusiastic software tester and .NET developer with a passion for exploring the world. With a diverse skill set and a love for both technology and travel, I bring a unique perspective to my work and personal experiences.

Software Testing:

I have a strong background in software testing, where I have honed my skills in ensuring the quality and reliability of software applications. Through various projects, I have gained expertise in creating and executing comprehensive test plans, performing various types of testing such as functional, integration, regression, and user acceptance testing. I am well-versed in test automation frameworks and tools, including Selenium and Appium, and I have a keen eye for identifying and reporting software defects. I strive to deliver exceptional software products by collaborating with development teams and providing valuable feedback for continuous improvement.

.NET Development:

Alongside my testing skills, I have a solid foundation as a .NET developer. I possess a deep understanding of the .NET framework and its associated technologies, allowing me to design and develop robust, scalable, and efficient software solutions. I have experience working with ASP.NET, C#, and SQL Server, crafting clean and maintainable code while following industry best practices. My knowledge of object-oriented programming and software architecture enables me to create elegant solutions that meet both functional and non-functional requirements.
